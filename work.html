Stuffed dates

This is great if you are craving something sweet. You can also serve this as a platter for you friends and family I know they'll enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • Dates
  • Stuffing peanut butter(prefrebly crunchy)
    whole nuts or
    coconut mixed with honey/maple syrup
  • Toppings pomergranate, melted chocolate, salt, zatar or other nuts

Directions

  • Step 1
    Get your dates and take out the seeds.
  • Step 2
    Fill each date with prefrable stuffing.
  • Step 3(optional)
    Get creative and decorate them as you wish.

Homemade popcorn

Feel like snacking but need something low in calories? Homemade popcorn is the solution

Ingredients

  • 100g corn kernals
  • 1tbspoil
  • Optional salt to taste

Directions

  • Step 1
    Add corn kernels into a pot (make sure the pot is dry).
  • Step 2
    Add the oil and cover the pot with a lid.
  • Step 3(optional)
    Put the stove on medium heat until you cant hear anymore popping corn. Turn off the stove, add salt to taste and enjoy
